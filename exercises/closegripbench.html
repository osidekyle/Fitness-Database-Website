Close Grip Bench Press

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
  • Bench
  • Weight Rack
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight while bar is on rack
  2. Lay down onto bench
  3. Grab bar just inside shoulder width
  4. Unrack weight
  5. Lower bar down to chest, keeping elbows tucked in
  6. Press bar back up away from chest until arms are locked out, and repeat until you have reached the desired amount of reps
