Barbell Bench Press

    Equipment Needed:
  • Bench
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load weight onto the bar
  2. Lay down on the bench
  3. Grasp bar at shoulder width
  4. Unrack weight
  5. Lower bar to chest, and press back up until elbows are locked out
  6. Repeat until desired number of repetition are completed
  7. Rerack weight
