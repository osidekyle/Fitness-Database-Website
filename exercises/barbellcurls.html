Barbell Bicep Curls

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
    Steps to perform:
  1. Pick bar up off grounds with palms facing away from you and hold at waist
  2. Bending only at the elbows, raise weight to chest
  3. Lower weight back down to waist
  4. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
