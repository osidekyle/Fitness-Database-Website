Seated Calf Raises

    Equipment Needed:
  • Seated Calf Raise Machine
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load machne with weight
  2. Sitting on machine, place feet and adjust pad
  3. Press into balls of feet until feet are fully extended
  4. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
