Barbell Rows

    Equipment Needed:
  • Dumbbells
  • Seat
    Steps to perform:
  1. Pick up dumbells and hold them at chest level with palms facing you
  2. Rotate arms outward until dumbells are outside of the shoulders
  3. Press dumbells overhead until arms are locked out
  4. Lower dumbells and rotate arms inward back to starting position
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
