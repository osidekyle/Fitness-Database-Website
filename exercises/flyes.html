Dumbbell Flyes

    Equipment Needed:
  • Bench
  • Dumbbells
    Steps to perform:
  1. Pick up dumbbells
  2. Sit on the edge of bench with dumbbells in hands
  3. Lay back onto bench and simultanesously move dumbbells over shoulders
  4. Press dumbbells to a straight arm positions
  5. Lower dumbbells and repeat motion for desired reps
  6. Once complete, simultaneously move dumbbells back down to knees as use sit up
