Dumbell Flyes

    Equipment Needed:
  • Bench
  • Dumbells
    Steps to perform:
  1. Pick up dumbells
  2. Sit on the edge of bench with dumbells in hands
  3. Lay back onto bench and simultanesously move dumbells over shoulders
  4. Press dumbells to a straight arm positions
  5. Lower dumbells and repeat motion for desired reps
  6. Once complete, simultaneously move dumbells back down to knees as use sit up
