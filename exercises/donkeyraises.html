Donkey Raises

    Equipment Needed:
  • Nothing!
    Steps to perform:
  1. Bend at hips until torso is parallel to floor
  2. Grab onto wall or other object with hands
  3. Press balls of feet into floor until heels raise off of ground and until foot is fully exgtneded
  4. Lower foot
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
