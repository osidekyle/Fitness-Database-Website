Barbell Calf Raises

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight and place bar on the ground
  2. Pick up the barbell
  3. Push into balls of feet until heels raise off of ground until foot is fully extended
  4. Lower heel back down to ground
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
