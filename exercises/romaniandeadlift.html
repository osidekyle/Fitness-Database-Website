Romanian Deadlifts

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight and place bar on the ground
  2. Pick up the barbell
  3. Keeping the back neutral, bend hips until bar is just below the knees
  4. Extend hips until body is ectended
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
