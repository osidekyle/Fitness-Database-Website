Barbell Rows

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
  • Weight Rack
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight on weight rack
  2. Pick up bar on the trapezius muscle and take a few steps back from the rack
  3. Bend hips and push hips back until torso is no more than parallel with the floor
  4. Push hips back forward and extend body
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
  6. Rerack weight
