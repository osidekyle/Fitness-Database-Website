Glute Ham Raises

    Equipment Needed:
  • Glute ham raise station
    Steps to perform:
  1. Place knees on pad, feet inside holders
  2. Extend knees until legs are straight
  3. Bend knees until knees form a 90 degree angle
  4. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
