Skullcrushers

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Bench
    Steps to perform:
  1. Pick up barbell from ground
  2. Sit on endge of bench
  3. Roll onto back and raise bar over shoulders
  4. While keeping elbows stationary, lower bar over face, bending only at the elbows
  5. Extend arms and repeat until desired number of reps are completed
