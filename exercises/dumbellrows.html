Dumbbell Rows

    Equipment Needed:
  • Dumbbell
  • Bench
    Steps to perform:
  1. Rest the knee and hand of the same side of the body on the bench, with dumbell laying next to bench
  2. Pick up the dumbbell
  3. Pull dumbell to lower chest, pulling elbows back and squeezing back
  4. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
