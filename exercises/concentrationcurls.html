Concentration Curls

    Equipment Needed:
  • Dumbbell
  • Bench
    Steps to perform:
  1. Sit on edge of bench with dumbbell on ground in front of you
  2. With palm facing up, grasp dumbbell and place elbow on the leg of the same side as the hand you are using
  3. Starting with the dumbbell down near the floor, bending only at elbow, raise dumbbell up towards chest
  4. Lower dumbbell back down towards floor and repeat until desired number of reps are completed
