Tricep Pushdowns

    Equipment Needed:
  • Cable Machine
    Steps to perform:
  1. Set cable above head
  2. Select weight
  3. Grab cable attachment and hold elbows at sides, with handsw up near face
  4. Bending only at the elbows, push cable downward until arms are extended
  5. Return cable to position near face and repeat until desired number of reps are completed
