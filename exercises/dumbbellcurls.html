Alternating Dumbbell Curls

    Equipment Needed:
  • Dumbells
    Steps to perform:
  1. Pick up dumbells and hold with palms out at waist
  2. On one side only, bend inly at the elbow to raise weight to chest
  3. Lower dumbbell to waist
  4. Alternate sides until desired number of reps are completed
