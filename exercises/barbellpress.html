Barbell Overhead Press

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
  • Weight Rack
    Steps to perform:
  1. Place bar on rack and load weight
  2. Pick up the barbell and hold up in front of your chest
  3. Press overhead until arms are fulling extended
  4. Lower bar back down to chest and repeat until desired number of reps are completed
