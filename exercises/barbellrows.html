Barbell Rows

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight and place bar on the ground
  2. Pick up the barbell
  3. Bend hips until torso is at a 45 degree angle with the floor
  4. Pull bar to lower chest while holding bent hip position, pulling elbows back and squeezing the back
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
