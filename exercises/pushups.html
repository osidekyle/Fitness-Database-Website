Pushups

    Equipment Needed:
  • Nothing!
    Steps to perform:
  1. Get on the ground, holding yourself up on your toes and hands
  2. Start with your body straight and flat, and arms locked out
  3. Bend arms until chest is just above the floor
  4. Push back up until your arms are locked out
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
  6. Repeat until desired number of repetition are completed
