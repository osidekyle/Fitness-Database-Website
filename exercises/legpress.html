Leg Press

    Equipment Needed:
  • Leg Press Machine
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load weight plates onto leg press machine
  2. Sit down in leg press machine and place feet shoulder width apart
  3. Unhook the weight and lower the weight until the knees form a 90 degree angle
  4. Push the weight back up, stopping before the knees lock out
  5. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
