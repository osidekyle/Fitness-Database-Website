Conventional Deadlift

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight and place bar on the ground
  2. Bend over and grab bar
  3. To set up the movement, keep back neutral while grabbing bar
  4. Push with legs, extend hips, and raise chest until barbell is at waist level and body is fulling extended
  5. Lower back onto ground and repeat until the desired number of reps is reached
