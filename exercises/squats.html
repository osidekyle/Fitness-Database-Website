Barbell Squats

    Equipment Needed:
  • Barbell
  • Weight Plates
  • Weight Rack
    Steps to perform:
  1. Load bar with weight with bar on rack
  2. Place bar on trapezius
  3. Unrack weight and take a few steps away from the rack
  4. Bracing your abdominals, bend at the knees and hips until the hip joint is below the top of the knee
  5. Push with the legs until legs are straight
  6. Repeat until desired number of reps are completed
